# Observer Mode

The player observes history rather than controlling it.

Core surfaces should include the World Chronicle, Jianghu Ledger, Imperial Register, Archive of Sects, Register of Clans, War Annals, Court Dossier, Rumours, True Record, Fate Record, and Abyssal Record.

Good observer features answer:

- What happened publicly?
- What truly happened?
- Who benefited?
- Which old grudge, debt, lineage, technique, or imperial policy caused it?
- Who knows the truth?
- Which rumours are false, incomplete, or deliberately planted?
